Volunteer With Us

United Assembly is volunteer-powered. Whether you have an hour a week or a full commitment, there's a role for you.

Why Volunteer

Make a Real Difference

Every bill we publish, every community we reach, and every person we help is made possible by volunteers. No experience in politics or law required — just a desire to see change.

🔬

Policy Research

Help us research issues, gather data, and build the evidence base for our model legislation.

✍️

Writing & Editing

Draft blog posts, plain-language explainers, and outreach materials that make policy accessible.

📣

Outreach & Canvassing

Represent United Assembly in your community, at events, and in schools.

💻

Web & Design

Help maintain our websites, create graphics, and build the digital presence of the organization.

🏫

Youth Education

Work with schools to establish student chapters and bring civic literacy programs to young people.

⚖️

Legal & Civil Support

Help connect community members with resources for navigating civil issues and legal challenges.
