What We Do

A Full-Spectrum Civic Organization

From bill research to community outreach, United Assembly works across every stage of the legislative and civic process.

📋

Policy Research

We study the issues that affect everyday Americans and develop evidence-based model legislation for advocates and lawmakers.

📝

Bill Development

Our team drafts comprehensive public bill proposals — like the Affordability Act — ready for introduction at state and federal levels.

📣

Public Awareness

We publish plain-language explainers, host public conversations, and help communities understand the issues shaping their lives.

🤝

Civil Outreach

We assist individuals and communities navigating civil issues, connecting them with resources and amplifying their voices.
