United Assembly is officially up and running. We're a nonprofit organization dedicated to researching, developing, and publishing model public legislation that addresses the real challenges facing Americans today.

## What We're Building

Our first major initiative — the **Affordability Act** — is already published and available at [affordact.com](https://affordact.com). It's a comprehensive proposal addressing the affordability crisis, from housing costs to everyday expenses.

But that's just the beginning. United Assembly exists to be a *factory of civic change* — producing well-researched bill proposals on a range of issues and getting them into the hands of advocates and lawmakers who can act on them.

## How to Get Involved

We're a volunteer-driven organization, and we're growing. Whether you're a researcher, writer, policy wonk, community organizer, or just someone who cares about making things better — there's a role for you.

Reach out at [contact@unitedassembly.us](mailto:contact@unitedassembly.us) or visit our [get involved](/contact/) page.
